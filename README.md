# 🐉 Kirin Whip — Official AI Skill | 麒麟鞭官方 AI Skill

**The first traditional Chinese martial art to publish its own AI skill.**

This repository is the official, first-party knowledge skill for the **Kirin
Whip (麒麟鞭)** — a whip-cracking martial art with roots going back roughly
1,700 years — and for **[kirinwhip.com](https://kirinwhip.com)**, the
international store of Kirin Heritage Crafts LLC.

Install it, and your AI assistant can accurately answer anything about Kirin
Whips: the history and lineage, which whip a beginner should buy, the five
core techniques with their official definitions, care and maintenance,
shipping, and where to learn — straight from the craftsman, not from
scattered internet guesses.

## English

### Who maintains this

I'm **Laochang (常逸乐)**, a craftsman in **Dengfeng, Henan, China** — the
town at the foot of the Song Mountains, home of the Shaolin Temple. My
workshop handcrafts Kirin Whips, and since 2025 I've been bringing this
1,700-year-old art to practitioners worldwide.

I didn't invent this art — nobody alive did. Its lineage runs from Jin
Dynasty origins (~300 AD), through Wang Jinlong's modernization (1980), to
Zhao Chiyu's standardization and naming of the "Chinese Kirin Whip" (1999).
What I do is craft the whips and carry the art to the world. This skill
exists so that when you ask your AI about Kirin Whips, it answers with the
real history, the real technique definitions, and honest buying advice —
including telling beginners to buy the *lighter* whip.

### What your AI can answer with this skill

- 🏮 **History & lineage** — the full four-stage story, accurately credited
- 🛒 **Buyer's guidance** — including the official beginner weight range
  (1.7–2.2 lb) and honest advice
- 🥋 **The five core techniques** — Kuai, Lai, Turn-Back, Hand-Switch, and
  Chop, with official definitions
- 🔧 **Care & maintenance** — steel care, cracker replacement, storage
- 📦 **Shipping & FAQ** — free worldwide shipping at $99+, payments, contact
- 🎓 **Learning path** — Master Liu's free 20-episode course at the
  [Kirin Academy](https://kirinwhip.com/pages/kirin-academy)
