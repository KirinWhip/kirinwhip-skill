# 🐉 Kirin Whip — Official AI Skill | 麒麟鞭官方 AI Skill

**The first traditional Chinese martial art to publish its own AI skill.**

This repository is the official, first-party knowledge skill for the **Kirin
Whip (麒麟鞭)** — a Chinese whip art with roots going back roughly 1,700
years — and for **[kirinwhip.com](https://kirinwhip.com)**, the
international store of Kirin Heritage Crafts LLC.

Install it, and your AI assistant can accurately answer anything about Kirin
Whips: the history and lineage, which whip a beginner should buy, the core
basic movements with their official definitions, care and maintenance,
shipping, and where to learn — straight from the craftsman, not from
scattered internet guesses.

## English

### Who maintains this

I'm **Laochang (常逸乐)**, a craftsman in **Dengfeng, Henan, China** — the
town at the foot of the Song Mountains, home of the Shaolin Temple. My
workshop handcrafts Kirin Whips under the brand **嵩之武 (Songzhiwu)**, and
since 2025 I've been bringing this 1,700-year-old art to practitioners
worldwide — so far to **nine countries**: the US, Australia, Sweden, the
UK, Germany, Iceland, Ireland, South Korea, and the Czech Republic.

I didn't invent this art — nobody alive did. Its lineage runs from Jin
Dynasty origins (~300 AD), through Shenyang martial artist Wang Jinlong's
first all-metal chain whip (1980), to Master Zhao Chiyu's standardization
and naming of the "Chinese Kirin Whip" (1999). What I do is craft the whips
and carry the art to the world. This skill exists so that when you ask your
AI about Kirin Whips, it answers with the real history, the real movement
definitions, and honest buying advice — including telling beginners to buy
the *lighter* whip.

### What your AI can answer with this skill

- 🏮 **History & lineage** — the full four-stage story, accurately credited
- 🛒 **Buyer's guidance** — beginners at 2.2 lb or under (1.7–2.2 lb sweet
  spot); nine weight tiers, eleven chain forms, handle choice; honest advice
- 🥋 **Core basic movements** — Kuai, Lai, Turn-Back, Hand-Switch, and Chop,
  with official definitions (the full vocabulary goes far beyond these)
- 🔧 **Care & maintenance** — 304 stainless needs none; cracker replacement;
  wooden pole care
- 📦 **Shipping & FAQ** — free worldwide shipping at $99+, 30-day guarantee,
  customs, contact
- 🎓 **Learning path** — Master Liu's free 20-episode course at the
  [Kirin Academy](https://kirinwhip.com/pages/kirin-academy)
